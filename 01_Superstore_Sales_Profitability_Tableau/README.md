This Tableau project analyzes sales performance, profitability, discounts, customer profitability, subcategory performance, and geographic sales trends using Tableau Superstore sample data.

The dashboard includes map based analysis, KPI cards, time series trends, sales versus profit analysis, and customer profitability ranking to help users understand where revenue and profit are performing well and where margin issues may need attention.

> **Data Note:** This project uses Tableau Superstore sample data for portfolio demonstration purposes. It does not contain private client data or real customer personal data.

## Business Problem

Business leaders need to understand how sales, profit, discounts, product categories, customer performance, and geographic regions are contributing to overall performance.

This dashboard answers questions such as:

* Which states and regions are generating the most sales and profit?
* Which customers are the most profitable?
* Which subcategories are contributing positively or negatively to profit?
* How are sales, profit, discounts, and profit ratio trending over time?
* Where are high sales not translating into strong profitability?
* Which areas may need pricing, discount, or profitability review?

## Dashboard Views

| View | Description |
|---|---|
| Sales by Location | Map view showing sales and profit by state |
| Sales vs Profit | Scatter plot comparing sales and profit by category, segment, and state |
| Customer Profitability | Ranked table showing highest profit customers |
| Subcategory Profitability | Profitability breakdown by product subcategory |
| Profits by Location | Dashboard combining map, scatter plot, and customer profitability |
| Key Metric Trends | Dashboard showing sales, profit, average discount, median discount, and profit ratio trends |

## Business Value

This dashboard helps business users quickly identify sales and profitability patterns across locations, customers, product categories, and time periods.

It can support decisions such as:

* Which regions need profitability review
* Which customers are driving the most profit
* Which subcategories may need pricing or discount review
* How sales and profit are trending over time
* Where discounting may be impacting profit ratio
* Which areas should be prioritized for deeper analysis
